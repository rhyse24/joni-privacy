JONI Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Overview

JONI is a mini game hub app. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights.

Data We Collect

JONI collects only the following data, stored locally on your device:

  • Game session history (scores, play duration, game title)
  • User progress (XP, level, streak, total games played)
  • App settings (language, sound, notification preferences)

No personal information is collected. No name, email address, phone number, or location is ever collected or required.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using on-device storage (Hive). We do not operate any servers. Your data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

JONI does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. The app does not contain advertisements.

Future updates may integrate Google Play Games Services for leaderboards. If added, this will be governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Permissions

JONI requests only the INTERNET permission, which may be used for future online features. No other device permissions are requested.

Children's Privacy

JONI does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The app is suitable for all ages and contains no user-generated content or social features that could expose children to risk.

Data Deletion

You can delete all locally stored data at any time from within the app: Profile → Settings → Reset Data. Uninstalling the app also permanently removes all stored data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
